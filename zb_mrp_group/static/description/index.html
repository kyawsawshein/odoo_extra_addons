MRP Group

Overview

In the production of a finished product, multiple BOMs and manufacturing orders may be involved.Odoo provides multi-level BOM configurations to handle this complexity.Introducing ZestyBeanz's MRP Group module, a powerful Odoo addon helps to grouping of BOMs and Manufacturing Orders (MOs).

Key Features

Configuration of unique groups with colour code.

Multiple groups for BOMs.

Automatic grouping of manufacturing orders based on BOM's MRP group.

This functionality streamlines the management of complex production processes by enabling easier identification and organization of BOMs and associated MOs.
